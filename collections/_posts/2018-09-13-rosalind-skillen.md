Rosalind Skillen is an investigative reporter. Her work has been published in The Detail, a Northern Ireland-based news and analysis website.

Her reporting covers a wide range of topics, including:

* Business
* Courts
* Criminal Justice
* Environment
* Health
* Policing
* Politics

Skillen's work provides in-depth analysis and investigation into critical issues in Northern Ireland.
